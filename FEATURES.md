# Product Features

FacturApp is built for people who need to invoice, share payment links, and keep billing organized.

## Core user features

- Create professional invoices quickly
- Edit invoices before sending them
- Manage clients, companies, and products in one place
- Export invoices to PDF
- Track invoice state across the billing lifecycle
- Support partial payments when a job is paid in stages
- Share payment links through common channels
- Use the app in English or Spanish

## Product surfaces

- Web app
- PWA
- Android TWA

## What the product is not

- Not a full accounting suite
- Not a bank
- Not a payroll system
- Not a source-code showcase

## Public principle

We keep feature explanations focused on what users can do, not on the implementation behind the product.
